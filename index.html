About Me

Arnab Paul - Photographer

Hey there, I'm Arnab Paul, a passionate photographer who loves capturing life's most precious moments. I'm an expert in portrait, wedding, food, and fashion photography and I put my heart and soul into every image I create.

With 6 years of experience in the industry, starting from covering events in college to handling multiple diverse projects today, I've developed a keen eye for detail and a deep understanding of my craft. Whether I'm capturing the beauty of a bride on her special day or the deliciousness of a perfectly plated dish, I'm always striving to create images that are both stunning and authentic.

My expertise lies particularly in food, portraits, and wedding photography.
